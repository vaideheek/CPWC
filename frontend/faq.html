Frequently Asked Questions

For Customers

Registration and log in

Can I use the site without a registration?

Yes, you can browse the services without registration, but to book a service or to contact a Service Provider registration is required.

How do I register on the platform?

Click on "Sign Up Today" at the top of the home page, enter your details (email and password), and verify your e-mail to start using the platform.

I registered as a customer, but I want to be a Service Provider. How can I become one?

If you go to the "Profile Settings", there is a section where you can provide your TAX number, which will automatically make you a Service Provider, and you will see all the functionalities connected to that role.

I am unable to log in. What can I do?

If you forgot your password, click on "Forgot Password" on the Login page. Enter the e-mail address you have signed up with, and we will send you a new password via e-mail. Afterwards we advise you to change that password in "Profile Settings".

Searching and booking services

How do I search for a Service Provider?

Use Our home page to filter by service, location, and date and time.

How do I book a Service Provider?

After you have found a suitable service for your needs, click "Book Now" on the service’s site, and confirm the booking by clicking "Proceed to pay", and pay with the payment method of your choice.

Can I cancel an appointment? How far in advance can I do it?

Yes, you can cancel an appointment. However, Service Providers may charge a cancellation fee (depending on their policies) if you cancel within 24 hours of the scheduled time.

Can I reschedule my appointment?

Yes, you can reschedule by accessing "My Bookings" and selecting a new available time slot. Your Service Provider will be notified immediately.

Can I see past bookings?

Yes, all completed bookings can be accessed under "My Bookings."

Payments

How much does it cost to use the site as a customer?

Using the site as a customer is free. You only pay for the services you book.

Can I pay after the service is done?

No, payments are made at the time of booking to secure your appointment.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards, Google Pay, and Apple Pay.

Can I get a refund if I cancel my appointment?

Refund policies depend on the Service Provider and the timing of your cancellation. Read the Provider’s profile for more details.

Are there any fees for canceling an appointment?

Some Service Providers may apply a cancellation fee if you cancel within 24 hours of the appointment. The cancellation policy will be shown at booking.

Security and reliability

Are my payment details secure?

Yes, we use encrypted payment gateways compliant with EU regulations.

Will my information be shared with third parties?

No, your information is kept private and secure, per GDPR standards.

How do I know the Service Provider is reliable?

Check their ratings and customer reviews.

What if the Service Provider does something wrong or damages my property? What can I do?

Contact the Service Provider directly to discuss the issue. If further assistance is needed, contact Customer Support for guidance (at akos1106@mailbox.unideb.hu).

Is there a warranty period after the service is completed?

Warranty periods vary by Service Provider, so it’s best to ask your Provider about any guarantees on their service prior to booking.

Communication and notifications

Will I receive confirmation for my booking?

Yes, you will receive e-mail confirmation with the booking details.

Can I get notifications on my phone?

No, at the moment we only send notifications through e-mails.

How can I contact my Service Provider?

You can call or e-mail the Service Provider directly through the contact information they have provided.

How can I contact Customer Support?

Go to "Contact Us" on the website to reach our Customer Support team via e-mail (at akos1106@mailbox.unideb.hu).

Will the Service Provider bring their own tools and materials needed for the service?

Most Service Providers provide their own tools, but if specific materials are needed, confirm details with them through their provided contact information before the appointment.

Can I leave a review after the service?

Yes, customers are invited to leave reviews after the service is completed.

What happens if the Service Provider cancels my appointment?

You’ll be notified immediately, and you can reschedule or book another provider (in this case you will be refunded based on the refund policies of the Service Provider).

For Service Providers

Registration and profile management

How do I register as a Service Provider on the platform?

Click on "Sign Up" and select the "Sign Up as a Service Provider" option to create your account as a Service Provider, by entering your TAX Number.

What information should I include in my profile?

Include a short description about yourself and the services you do, and photos of your previous works.

Can I list multiple services?

Yes, you can add each service individually, complete with unique pricing and descriptions.

Can I add photos to my service listings?

Yes, upload photos to showcase your work and attract more customers.

Availability and booking management

How can I set my availability?

Use the calendar tool on your dashboard to set or update your available hours.

Can I manage my bookings through the platform?

Yes, you can view, reschedule, or cancel bookings through the provider dashboard.

Can I block off time for vacations or unavailability?

Yes, you can easily block off specific dates or times in your availability settings.

Can I cancel my appointment? How far in advance can I do it?

Yes, you can cancel an appointment through the platform. It’s recommended to do so as early as possible to minimize the impact on customers.

Are there any consequences if I cancel or reschedule my appointments repeatedly?

Frequent cancellations may impact your reputation on the platform. Avoid cancellations to maintain good standing.

Can I decline a job if I don’t feel qualified enough for it?

Yes, it’s best for both parties to decline jobs that are outside your expertise to maintain quality and customer satisfaction. Your client will get a full refund.

Payments and fees

Is the site free to use?

Registration to the site is free as a Service Provider, but we take a 10% cut from every booking you make through our site.

How do I get paid for my services?

Payments are processed securely and transferred to the bank account you provided.

How often are payments processed?

Payments are processed after the service is completed, and you will see the money in your bank account about 1-2 hours after the customer has paid through our system.

Can I get the invoices of the fees I pay with each booking?

Yes, invoices for the platform fees are available in your account under “Billing”.

Communication and customer interaction

How do I receive notifications about new bookings?

Through e-mail notifications.

Can customers contact me outside of this site?

Yes, through the phone number and e-mail address you have provided your customers can contact you.

Can I communicate with customers before appointments?

Yes, if the potential future customers have contacted you through your phone number, e-mail address, feel free to answer their questions before they commit to hire You as their Service Provider.

How can I contact the customer who hired me on the site?

Use either the phone number or e-mail address (based on which contact information the Customer has provided us with) which you can see in your email notification of the booking to communicate with customers regarding job specifics.

How do I respond to customer reviews?

Go to your reviews section to respond to feedback.

Can negative feedback be deleted from my profile if I think it isn’t fair?

If you feel like a feedback is unfair or violates guidelines, you can flag it for review by the platform’s support team. We will contact both you and the customer for further information and based on that, we will either ask the customer to edit the feedback, or leave it as is.

How do I report inappropriate reviews?

Flag any reviews that violate guidelines, and the Customer Support team will review them.

Policies and cancellations

How do I handle customer cancellations?

You’ll receive a notification via e-mail, and the booking status will update automatically on our site.

Can I set custom cancellation policies?

Yes, define your policies in the settings for customers to view before booking.

What happens if I accidentally damage the customer's property?

Immediately inform the customer and discuss any necessary actions. If needed, contact Customer Support for assistance.

Do I need insurance to offer services?

It’s recommended, but requirements depend on local regulations.

For everyone

Support and technical issues

I can’t log in. What can I do?

Click "Forgot Password" to reset your password. We will send you a new password to the e-mail address you provide. If the issue persists, contact customer support for assistance.

How do I contact Customer Support?

Go to "Contact Us" on the website to reach our Customer Support team via e-mail (at akos1106@mailbox.unideb.hu).

Is customer support available 24/7?

Yes, we will reply to your e-mails within 20-30 minutes.

Account and personal information

How is my personal information protected?

We follow GDPR guidelines and use data encryption to keep your information safe and secure.

How can I update my contact information or address?

Go to "Profile Settings" to update your contact information and other personal details.

How can I delete my account? What happens to my data if I do so?

You can delete your account in "Profile Settings". All personal data will be permanently removed from our servers following GDPR guidelines, within the next 7 days.
